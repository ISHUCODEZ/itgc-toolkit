PILLAR 3 · OPERATIONS & MONITORING

User-access recertification tracker

Access that was appropriate at provisioning can drift over time, so it must be re-reviewed on a cadence. This tool takes the population of access grants with their last-review dates and shows which are current, due soon, overdue, or never reviewed — the monitoring control that proves access stays appropriate.

⬆ Drop an access-review CSV here, or click to browse Columns: user, system, access_level, last_reviewed (date). Or run the bundled sample.
