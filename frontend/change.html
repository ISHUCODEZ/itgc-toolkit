PILLAR 2 · CHANGE MANAGEMENT

Change-management log auditor

Reads a change-ticket export and tests every change that reached production against the core change controls: authorisation (was it approved?), testing (is there test evidence?), and segregation (is the deployer independent of the developer?). Each failed check becomes a documented exception.

⬆ Drop a change-ticket CSV here, or click to browse Columns: change_id, developer, deployer, approved, tested, type, status. Or run the bundled sample.
